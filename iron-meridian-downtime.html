📘 Iron Meridian – Downtime Activity Guide

Choose what your character does between missions. Keep it fun, useful, and preferably non‑explosive.

⏱️ Quick Start & House Rules

Post in #downtime using the template Spend more days? Roll twice per check → take best Batch craft up to 4 identical items in 4 days Single craft often 2 days (1 day on crit) GM may award small 1‑week boons for great scenes

🎓 Train a Skill / Feat

  • Time: Usually 1 week per rank/feat (or as announced)
  • Roll: Relevant skill; trainer or study can add +1–+2
  • Perks: New proficiencies, languages, or Lore
Bonding with a familiar/companion can use Arcana/Occultism/Nature depending on method.

🧪 Crafting (Potions, Gear, Oddities)

  • Time: Batch of up to 4 identical items = 4 days • Single item = 2 days (1 day on crit)
  • Roll: Crafting / Alchemical Crafting vs item DC (pay 50% materials)
  • House Boons: Extra days → 2 rolls per check, keep best • Some crits can yield +1 bonus item
Example: Invisibility Potion (Lvl 4) DC 18, cost 5 gp; strong rolls may speed things up.

📖 Research & Rituals

  • Roll: Arcana, Occultism, Religion, Society, or Lore
  • Use: Identify curses, decode relics, design or perform rituals, dig up hooks
  • Outcomes: Clues, boons, or quest leads (e.g., soul‑communion successes granting weekly buffs)

🛠️ The Cage (Armoury) & Iron Reserve

  • Roll: Diplomacy (haggle), Crafting (repairs), Society (contacts)
  • Use: Appraisals, orders, stock work, gear swaps, paying tabs (Branna smiles when you do)
  • Note: Ask before touching anything that hums, glows, or judges you.

🩺 Clinics, Blessings & Hex‑Breaking

  • Roll: Medicine (treat wounds), Religion (blessings), Occultism (remove hex / counteract)
  • Use: Earn small coin, favors, or local goodwill
  • Perk: Exceptional service can grant tokens (free lodging, +1 to first social check in that town)

🤝 Social & Team Training (2‑Day Pack)

  • Day 1: Welcome tour + tavern bonding → +1 Diplomacy with guildmates (1 week)
  • Day 2: Yard drills with Yari → +1 to first attack next mission
Yes, stories count as training if they end with “and that’s why we check for traps.”

🕵️ Quiet Work (Sneak, Scout, Schmooze)

  • Roll: Stealth, Thievery, Deception, Society
  • Use: Favors, rumors, or “alternative solutions”
  • Warning: Complications can spawn side‑quests. You’ve been warned (again).

🦇 Bonds & Oddities

  • Familiar/Companion Bonding: Arcana/Occultism/Nature → obedience boosts, tricks, quirky boons
  • Strange Items: Ask Solen to identify before it hatches/explodes/judges you
  • House Boon: Great bond scenes can grant 1‑week minor bonuses
If your chicken lays a glowing egg, that’s a feature, not a bug.

Not seeing your idea listed? Pitch it! If it’s cool and doesn’t implode the tavern, we’ll figure it out.
