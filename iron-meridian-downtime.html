📘 Iron Meridian – Downtime Activity Guide

Choose what your character does between missions!

🎓 Train a Skill / Feat

  • Use: 1 week per skill rank or feat
  • Benefit: Improve a skill, learn a new language or Lore
  • Requires: Access to a trainer and gold (ask the DM)

🪙 Earn Income

  • Use: Roll a relevant skill (Crafting, Performance, Lore, etc.)
  • Benefit: Gain gold based on roll and level

🔧 Crafting

  • Use: Crafting check vs. item DC (4 day minimum)
  • Benefit: Make gear cheaper than buying it
  • Requires: Crafting skill, materials, tools, recipe

📖 Research

  • Use: Arcana, Occultism, Religion, Society, or Lore
  • Benefit: Learn info, hints, or side quest leads

🛍️ Trade / Haggle

  • Use: Diplomacy, Deception, or Intimidation
  • Benefit: Trade gear, get discounts, unlock secret stock

🤫 Sneaky Stuff

  • Use: Stealth, Thievery, Deception, Society, etc.
  • Benefit: Gain favors, info, or “alternative solutions”
  • Note: Might lead to new complications

❤️ Personal Projects / Roleplay

  • Use: Any skill or story-based idea
  • Benefit: Progress goals, unlock new plot, earn rep or gifts

Need help? Just ask the GM — we'll figure it out together!
