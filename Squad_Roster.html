Squads of the Iron Meridian

Not every contract is handed to a loose pile of hopeful mercenaries and a prayer. Some jobs go to established squads with reputations sharp enough to cut paper. These are the crews people whisper about in taverns, on battlements, and occasionally while hiding under tables.

The Stone Vultures

Guild Squad Elite Gold Rank Active

A squad forged from mercenaries who saw too many campaigns, the Stone Vultures are called in when jobs are too dangerous or delicate for anyone else. They circle over enemies like carrion birds: patient, merciless, and certain.

MembersCaptain Dregor Marr, Vesha the Silent, Garruk Stonehide, Lady Ilyra, Thorne of the Eastmarch
Notable TraitsCold, Efficient, Feared
Best Used ForHigh-risk strikes, brutal clean-up, and jobs where subtlety arrives wearing steel boots
Guildmaster Nil says: “When the Vultures fly, someone’s already dead. They’re just waiting for the body to fall.”

🍻 Tavern Rumors

  • They never leave a body behind, for reasons best not asked.
  • Ilyra doesn’t just cast illusions. She collects memories.
  • They’ve turned down contracts before. Means they’re not desperate. Means they’re dangerous.

The Ashen Blades

Guild Squad Veteran Silver Rank Active

The Ashen Blades earned their name in the Harrowing, where they marched through fire and ruin. They’re professional, efficient, and terrifying in the field. Not known for mercy, but always for results.

MembersSer Calvess, Tiora Emberborn, Korr Blacktusk, Silas Drowe
Notable TraitsRuthless, Battle-Hardened, Fire-Scarred
Best Used ForHard pushes, direct assaults, and anything improved by smoke, steel, or bad decisions made confidently
Guildmaster Nil says: “They burn bridges as easily as they burn their foes. Keep them pointed outward.”

🍻 Tavern Rumors

  • You can tell where they’ve been, just follow the smoke.
  • They don’t drink, they smolder.
  • Calvess is still haunted by the family he failed. That sword of his whispers.

The Gilded Geese

Guild Squad Eccentric Bronze Rank Active

The Gilded Geese are equal parts adventurers and spectacle. They strut, they boast, and somehow they keep surviving fights that should have killed them. Their symbol, a golden goose feather, is pinned to every cloak and usually sparkling with some minor glamour.

MembersDame Ophelia Proudwing, Krixle Feathercap, Hark Dander, Fenno & Jilli
Notable TraitsFlashy, Lucky, Unpredictable
Best Used ForOdd errands, social chaos, and missions where charm is either essential or deeply unhelpful
Guildmaster Nil says: “They’re ridiculous. They’re also still alive. Make of that what you will.”

🍻 Tavern Rumors

  • They once conned a noble into paying them twice for the same job.
  • Ophelia sings before battle. The enemy sometimes just walks away.
  • Lucky? Or cursed? Depends on who you ask.

The Rust Rats

Guild Squad Mercenary Bronze Rank Active

The Rust Rats make a living off the scraps, both literally and figuratively. Known for fighting with whatever’s lying around, they turn junk into weapons and salvage into gold. Their missions often end with more scrap brought back than coin earned.

MembersTalla Quickfingers, Brusk Ironjaw, Pimmle Dottle, Ryn of the Rustfields
Notable TraitsScrappy, Improvised, Loyal
Best Used ForSalvage runs, messy survival work, and anything involving broken walls, missing tools, or terrible odds
Guildmaster Nil says: “They may smell like an iron heap, but you’ll want them on your side when the walls come down.”

🍻 Tavern Rumors

  • They once held off a demon with a broken chair leg and a soup pot.
  • Never leave your tools unattended, they’ll find a better use for them.
  • They’re saving up to buy proper gear. Been saying that for two years.
No squads match that filter. Which is awkward, because the guild swears they exist.
