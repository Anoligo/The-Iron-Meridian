The Iron Meridian – Guild Overview

✨ World Background Summary

Current Year: 2050 AD (After Divide)

A dying god tore the world apart 4 000 years ago in The Divide. Since then: undead empires, dragon kings, and the mini-apocalypse The Harrowing, which left glowing demon rifts everywhere.

Old powers are dust; heroes are scarce; mercenaries thrive. Enter The Iron Meridian, the fort-dwelling guild that punches nightmares, takes contracts, and sometimes argues over magic boots.

🏰 Structure & Rules

Base: Ancient keep on demon-scarred land. Cheap rent, great ambience, constant risk.

Mission Access: Recommended ranks exist. Trolls can’t read recommendations.

Loot: Offer finds to the Armoury for appraisal ➜ gold. Honest rates (mostly).

Training: Missed a mission? Away-training ensures you return wiser and only slightly rusty.

Guild Flow: Members rotate — some off on side quests, others home mending wounds. Pretend they never left… unless they bring snacks.

🥇 Guild Rewards & Away Progression

  • XP: Active players earn XP; absent players receive the average — whether training, undercover, or on an epic lunch break.
  • Merits: Awarded for heroics (or not exploding the healer). Spend on perks and upgrades.

🏦 Guild Armoury

  • Turn in loot ➜ 1 : 1 gold. No haggling.
  • Short on coin? Rent an item for a session at 20 % of market price — break it, buy it.
  • Stock rotates; some gear vanishes forever. Don’t get too attached to that flaming crossbow.

🏛️ Modular Mission Structure

Session hooks come from:

  • 📜 External contracts (towns, nobles, trade routes)
  • 🛡️ Internal guild needs (demon incursions, training drills)
  • 🕵️ Faction intrigue (covert tasks, diplomacy)

Choose missions to fit the group’s time and mood.

📘 Pathfinder 2e – Quick Rules Guide

⚔️ Runes (Weapons & Armour)

Runes improve gear in two ways:

  • Fundamental Runes: Potency (+1 to +3), Striking (weapons), Resilient (armour)
  • Property Runes: Extra effects (Flaming, Frost, etc)

Property rune limit = Potency bonus (e.g. +2 weapon = 2 property runes)

Rune TierLevelCraft DCTypical Cost
+1 / Striking2–416–1830–100 gp

Failing a crafting check wastes time. Only critical failure loses materials (~10%).

🛡️ Armour Class (AC)

AC = 10 + Proficiency + Dex modifier + Armour bonus + Item bonus + other bonuses

  • Proficiency = Level + (Trained +2 / Expert +4 / Master +6 / Legendary +8)
  • Armour may limit max Dex bonus
  • Potency runes increase item bonus

🎯 Attack Rolls (To Hit)

Attack = d20 + Proficiency + Ability modifier + Item bonus + other bonuses

  • Melee → Strength (usually)
  • Ranged → Dexterity (usually)
  • Potency runes add to hit

✨ Spell DC & Spell Attack

Spell DC = 10 + Proficiency + Casting Ability modifier + Item bonus

Spell Attack = d20 + Proficiency + Casting Ability modifier + Item bonus

  • Wizard → Intelligence
  • Cleric / Druid → Wisdom
  • Sorcerer / Bard → Charisma
