Branna’s Bakefire Biscuits

Consumable, Common, Alchemical, Food
Level: 1     Price: 4 sp (per biscuit)     Bulk: L

Description

A coarse, dense biscuit made with hardy flour, dried berries, smoked marrow fat, and powdered rootstock. Hard enough to be used as a bludgeoning tool in a pinch. Popular among Iron Meridian mercs on long missions due to its shelf life and stamina-boosting properties.

When eaten, it grants a subtle morale boost and reinforces grit for a short time.

Activate [Interact]

Effect: Gain a +1 item bonus to saving throws against fatigue and fear effects for 1 hour.

  • If consumed as part of Breakfast before an adventure (downtime), also grants a +1 bonus to Endurance-related checks (forced march, swimming, long shifts).
  • Effects don’t stack — but biscuits do in your gut.

Craft Requirements

Crafting DC: 15

  • Hardy flour or hardtack base
  • Bone marrow fat or salted lard
  • Dried herbs or berries
  • Binding rootstock or starch
  • Flame-hot oven or forge stone

Traits: Alchemical, Consumable, Food, Comfort

Crafting Time: 1 hour per batch (makes 3 biscuits)

Special: A failed Crafting check still yields edible, but ineffective, biscuits. A critical success produces biscuits that also grant 1d8 healing.
