📜 Iron Meridian - Mission Report

Mission:

🧠 Guild Priority: Echo Containment (Outpost Theta)

Participants:

  • Buggie
  • Ecaon
  • ElderDan
  • Vokkar
  • Styx-Red-Claw
  • ObsidianIvy
  • Tink-Tonk the Cogsmith (Fallen in action)

Summary:

The team infiltrated the Ivory Labyrinth’s re-education hub in the Ashminster Verge and disabled the Mnemosyne Relay, halting a psychic broadcast that was hollowing minds for demonic ingress. High Templar Hylas was captured alive. Archivist Dalia and multiple civilians were freed from harmonic control. The victory came at a cost: Tink-Tonk fell during the final push, his last instructions enabling the relay’s destruction.

Highlights:

  • Covert Entry: A hidden service tunnel let the team bypass the main gate patrols.
  • Runic Lock: ObsidianIvy’s “accidental” pipe alignment revealed the solution path; the door yielded.
  • Psychic Construct: Tink-Tonk spotted a leg-joint weakness; Vokkar bowled it over; a coordinated finish by Vokkar and Styx ended the threat.
  • Echo Chamber: Harmonic haze navigated; Archivist Dalia roused; Lazslo helped escort civilians out.
  • Beacon Room: The relay’s pulse turned half the party on Tink-Tonk under Hylas’s command. With his final breath, he shouted the weak point—“the crystal core.” The team shattered it, dropping Hylas and freeing allies.
  • Aftermath: Hylas taken prisoner; civilians recovered; Tink-Tonk’s body borne home with honors.

Outcome:

Outpost Theta neutralized; Mnemosyne Relay destroyed; psychic pressure in the Verge subsided within minutes. Hylas secured for interrogation. Civilians and Archivist Dalia stabilized. Guild success—with loss.

Reward:

  • 💰 1600 gp
  • 📚 Psionic Archive Tier I access (Occultism +1 vs. Aberrations/psychic topics while referencing the stacks)
  • 🗡️ Hylas’s Blade — +1 Striking Bastard Sword (grim, black styling)
  • 🛡️ Mind-Shielding Amulet ×2 — +1 item bonus to Will saves vs. mental (invested)
  • 🔧 Ethereal Capacitor (spent; 25 gp as components if sold)
  • 🎧 Aural Dampeners ×4 (reusable guild issue)

Next Steps:

Interrogate Hylas; trace supply and signal lines for other relay nodes; prioritize counter-operations under Operation Iron Veil. Armoury to audit and archive Tink-Tonk’s designs.

Guildmaster’s Comment:

“You did more than asked. The beacon is dead, the High Templar breathes Guild air in chains. That’s a win. But this—this was not in the plan. Tink-Tonk was a menace and a marvel. He liked working with you. He trusted you. We’ll give him a proper send-off. Rest now. Tomorrow, the war resumes.”
