📜 Iron Meridian - Mission Report

Mission:

🧪 Whispers from the Chimera Vault

Participants:

  • Vokkar
  • Nyx
  • Vex
  • Ed
  • Nymeria

Summary:

Acting on leads tied to the missing Grey Chain courier Lyra Shardfoot, the squad descended into the sealed Chimera Vaults beneath Ashminster. With help from Rusty Malik and a suspiciously dubious copied key, they breached the vault systems, fought through hybrid guardians, recovered Lyra alive from an active surgical vat, and secured the sought-after chimera serum.

The deeper they pushed, the clearer it became that the long-dead experiments of the vault had not merely resumed, but flourished under the hand of Arik Thorne, a renegade surgeon bent on “improving” life through mutation. The squad’s progress included tactical successes, one extremely ill-advised explosives plan, a sprinting retreat from a fire-breathing chimera, and a final confrontation in Thorne’s vat chamber that ended with the surgeon thrown back into the reach of his own monstrous creation.

With Thorne dead, the serum recovered, Lyra rescued, and a collection of oddly cooperative mutated creatures in tow, the squad emerged from the vaults victorious—if not especially subtle.

Highlights:

  • Rusty’s Lead: Vokkar confirmed the mission with Rusty Malik, who promised one custom-crafted weapon if Lyra returned alive and supplied the copied key and best point of entry.
  • Vault Entry: Nyx identified the lock’s magical component; Ed and Vex determined the key required a spell injection to function. The door consumed the magic, sealed behind the party, and ejected the key.
  • First Contact: A nearby trap released a swarm of hybrid monkey-dogs and a dragon-hog variant, swiftly dispatched before the group pressed deeper.
  • Courier Trail: In the Chimera Pens, Nymeria recovered Lyra’s satchel and a note confirming she had hidden the vial.
  • Splice Laboratory: The squad breached an occupied surgical theatre, defeated a mutated brute, resisted hostile injections, and coerced the resident surgeon into explaining how to safely free Lyra.
  • Lyra Recovered: The team released Lyra successfully, returned her satchel, and recovered the serum. She was escorted to safety with the key and instructions to return to Rusty.
  • The Hatchery Incident: After discovering eggs and a strange frog-like creature, the squad elected to solve the matter with explosives. This destroyed the room, the eggs, and nearly the squad when a dragon-like hybrid burst through the blast site in fury.
  • Maintenance Tunnels: The team escaped through a maintenance hatch after enduring a devastating cone of flame, later discovering holding cells containing several captive hybrid creatures.
  • Final Chamber: Arik Thorne attempted a monologue and an offer of “progress.” Vokkar objected by charging him immediately. Thorn manifested mutagenic tentacles and was eventually denied escape through a combination of blocking, grappling, command magic, shield-bashing, and one final strike from Nyx.
  • Monster Justice: Thorn’s own released prototype finished reducing him to pulp after the squad hurled him back into its reach.
  • Aftermath: The team discovered Thorne’s secret cache, personal research notes, a mutagen injector, and enough gold to make the whole ordeal feel slightly more professional. Peaceful creatures were released and escorted out through an upper maintenance route.

Outcome:

Mission success. Lyra Shardfoot recovered alive, chimera serum secured, Arik Thorne eliminated, and active experimentation within the Chimera Vaults disrupted. Several mutated captives and creatures were freed during extraction. The vault complex remains dangerous, unstable, and still full of questions best answered from a safe distance.

Reward:

  • 💰 1,200 gp
  • ⛓️ Favor from the Grey Chain
  • 🛠️ One custom-crafted weapon from Rusty Malik (promised upon Lyra’s safe return)
  • 🧪 Chimera serum secured for Guild study
  • 📓 Thorne’s research notes
  • 💉 Thorne’s Mutagen Injector
  • 💰 Hidden gold cache and assorted experimental materials

Next Steps:

Deliver Thorne’s notes to Guild analysts and Archivist review. Determine what can safely be learned from the recovered serum before anyone drinks it “just to see.” Clarify references to the Grey Benefactor and the Empty Chorus. Also determine whether Vex is now legally responsible for the frog creature.

Guildmaster’s Comment:

“You found the courier, brought down the butcher running the place, and somehow came back with the serum, the notes, and a trail of liberated monstrosities. That counts as success. Next time, though, when you find a room full of eggs and unstable chemicals, I’d like you to at least pretend explosives weren’t your first thought.”
