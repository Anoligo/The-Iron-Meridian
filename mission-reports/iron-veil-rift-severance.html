⚔️ Iron Meridian - Mission Report

Mission:

🌌 Guild Priority: Iron Veil — Rift Severance

Squad:

  • The Stone Vultures

Location:

Shadowlands Rift, Primary Caldera

Threat Level:

Gold Rank | Level 9 | Extreme

Objective:

  • Deploy ward pylons
  • Seize keystone obelisk
  • Defeat Rift Warden
  • Complete counter-ritual
  • Survive backlash

Summary:

The Stone Vultures entered the Primary Caldera under the shadow of an impossible truth: the Rift was not unstable—it was being held open.

Initial insertion met immediate resistance. Rift-spawned entities surged toward the caldera rim, drawn not by chaos—but by structure. The Blades advanced with deliberate brutality, establishing ward pylons under sustained assault.

At the heart of the caldera, the Keystone Obelisk pulsed with controlled energy, anchoring the Rift. The squad split formation—one element holding the perimeter while the others breached the obelisk’s defenses. Destruction of the structure triggered a violent destabilisation, causing the Rift to fluctuate for the first time since discovery.

The Rift Warden emerged shortly after.

Reports describe it not as a creature—but as a convergence of form and void. It did not attack immediately. It corrected.

Engagement was prolonged and catastrophic. Terrain degradation, reality distortion, and intermittent gravitational collapse were observed. The Blades adapted. They always do.

The Warden was destroyed.

The Rift did not collapse.

Following prior intelligence provided by Vokkar, the squad initiated the counter-ritual only after confirming destabilisation thresholds. Timing proved critical. The ritual succeeded.

The Rift collapsed inwards.

The backlash followed.

All members of the The Stone Vultures withdrew during partial planar collapse. Severe environmental damage recorded. Caldera stability compromised. Residual anomalies expected for several days.
