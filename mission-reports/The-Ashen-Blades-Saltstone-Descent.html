📜 Iron Meridian - Mission Report

Mission:

The Saltstone Descent

Squad:

The Ashen Blades (Silver Rank)

  • Silas Drowe — Scout & Cartographer
  • Korr Blacktusk — Vanguard Greatsword
  • Tiora Emberborn — Pyromancer
  • Ser Calvess — Sword-Saint

Type / Duration / Status / Reward:

  • Exploration • Combat • Puzzle
  • ⏳ Medium–Long
  • ✅ Completed
  • 💰 600 gp + Ancient Seals (bonus marks of dwarven favor)

Objective:

  • Reach the sealed dwarven archive beneath the Saltstone Catacombs.
  • Map tunnels for Guild records.
  • Bypass ancient defenses.
  • Collect dwarven stone-tablets.
  • Return alive.

Summary:

Descent & Mapping. The Ashen Blades entered via a collapsing mineshaft, ash and old smoke still clinging to the supports. Silas mapped precise turns while Korr carved physical markers. Pockets of poisonous salt-fog scoured lungs and steel.

Puzzles of Stone & Salt. At the first vault, three dwarven reliefs concealed salt-crystal lenses. Tiora’s heat nearly spider-cracked one, but her controlled flame lit a hidden glyph that revealed the pattern. Ser Calvess forced the final alignment, grinding stone rings until the door groaned open.

Combat in the Catacombs. Salt-ghouls (brine-twisted dwarven dead) ambushed the squad. Korr cleaved a rank with a single arc, Tiora’s blasts turned the tunnels into a furnace, and Calvess’ blade “whispered” with each strike. Silas took a shallow claw wound but pressed on.

The Rivalry Below. In the archive vault, a rival mercenary company already had the tablets in hand. A tense parley followed. Calvess refused to yield; Tiora pointed out the roof’s ominous settling. The rivals offered terms: they take the tablets; the Blades take the seals. The Blades—calculating the odds—agreed.

The Collapse. Saltstone began to shear and fall. Korr shouldered a beam long enough for the others to scramble out. They escaped scarred by ash and brine, map complete, seals secured—while the archive entombed itself behind them.

Outcome:

  • ✅ Archive reached; tunnels mapped; defenses bypassed.
  • ⚠️ Primary objective (Tablets): Lost to rival squad via negotiated concession.
  • 🏺 Secondary reward (Ancient Seals): Secured.
  • ⛏️ Catacombs collapsed—future recovery now improbable.

Consequences:

  • Rival squad gains immediate dwarven favor; the Blades earn grudging respect for surviving and returning with Seals.
  • Guild archives expanded with a full Saltstone map package.
  • Reputation note: “Ruthless survivors” who can read a falling ceiling.

Rewards Issued:

  • 💰 600 gp (squad payout).
  • 🪙 3× Ancient Dwarven Seals — artifact-value markers useful in dwarven negotiations; redeemable for Guild Credit when invoked.
  • 📈 +1 Guild Reputation for mapping under hostile conditions.

MVP:

🏆 Korr Blacktusk — Held the failing beam and kept the exit open while the team evacuated; without that stand, there would be no report to file.
