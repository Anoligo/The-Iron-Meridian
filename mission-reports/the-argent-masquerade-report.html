📜 Iron Meridian - Mission Report

Mission:

🎭 The Argent Masquerade (Ashminster Highcourt) — Infiltration & Asset Protection

Participants:

  • Vokkar
  • ObsidianIvy (with familiar, Laszlo)
  • Ecaon
  • Vexara
  • Nyx

Summary:

Operatives infiltrated the Ashminster Highcourt gala to unmask an Ivory Labyrinth agent and safeguard the relic known as the Heart of the Highcourt. Entry was achieved via distraction (Vexara’s impromptu “musician” routine) and concealment, after which the team mapped guest movements, cross-checked a stolen guest list, and eavesdropped on whispers surrounding a suspicious Cask 17.

A paralytic wine plot was uncovered in the cellars; ObsidianIvy proved the recipe by identifying components and carving a stabilizing rune to revive a “test subject” (Vokkar) after a deliberate feint. Evidence pointed first to poet Seren Kesh, who confessed under duress that his family had been taken and he’d been forced to dose the wine. Pursuit led to a secret vault where Valerius revealed himself as the true agent, supported by unseen templars.

Valerius attempted to bargain for the Heart, then escalated—deploying a crystalline prism to dominate Ecaon. Vokkar severed the control; the templars were brought down; Valerius was captured alive. The team secured the Heart and staged the scene for misdirection during exfiltration.

Highlights:

  • Covert Entry: Vexara’s gate performance distracted guards while the squad slipped inside under invisibility.
  • Paper Trail: Guest list cross-check in the library; handwriting sample from Seren’s poem did not match the ritual book.
  • Cask 17: Cellar workers grumbled; testing confirmed a masquerade sleep draught in the vintage—labels swapped and cask neutralized.
  • Hedge & Stables: Seren went missing; tracked and taken without raising the ballroom alarm.
  • The Vault: Valerius unmasked; invisible templars engaged; Mind-Control Prism shattered when Vokkar removed the wielder’s hand (cleanly, even).
  • Extraction: Heart secured; Captain Jorn silenced to protect the operation; exit executed without public panic.

Outcome:

  • Ivory Labyrinth operation at the Highcourt exposed and thwarted.
  • Seren KeshSeren Kesh turned over to Ashminster authorities as a coerced pawn. For now he remains under guard, awaiting judgment — though the Guild has pledged to aid him in recovering his family, should the chance arise.
  • Valerius captured alive for interrogation.
  • Heart of the Highcourt retrieved and transferred to Guild custody.
  • House security compromised but contained; civilian casualties avoided.

Loot & Notable Items:

  • Heart of the Highcourt: Crystalline relic attuned to the ambitions/desires of nearby minds.
  • Masquerade Sleep Draught Recipe: Formula and cellar notes for a paralytic wine-dosing potion.
  • Mind-Control Prism (shattered): Crystalline focus formerly used for short-term domination.

Reward:

  • 💰 1000g distributed among participants
  • 🏛 Political Leverage: secured discreet favor with Lord Renn, a notorious but well-connected drunkard, who remembers the operatives all too fondly from the gala

Next Steps:

  • Interrogate Valerius (network, handlers, Host cells inside the Highcourt).
  • Locate and secure Seren Kesh’s family; evaluate amnesty terms.
  • Heart of the Highcourt: full appraisal, warding, and ethics review under Solen Telvair.

Guildmaster’s Comment:

“Masks hide faces; coin hides motives. You tore through both without lighting the ballroom on fire—commendable restraint. Valerius breathes, the Heart is ours, and Ashminster still thinks it was a splendid evening. That’s a win in three languages. Debrief at dawn. Wear something less shiny.”
